03 · KEY FEATURES
What CMAF brings to streaming
fMP4 unification

CMAF standardizes on fragmented MP4 as the one container format for both HLS and DASH. Each fMP4 segment is self-contained and independently decodable — compatible with both .m3u8 and .mpd delivery layers without re-packaging.

Chunked transfer & low latency

CMAF introduced chunked transfer encoding — segments are broken into smaller chunks delivered over HTTP/2 as they're encoded. Players start playing a chunk before the full segment exists. Combined with LL-HLS and LL-DASH: under 2 seconds end-to-end.

Common Encryption (CENC)

One encryption pass embeds multiple DRM license headers — FairPlay for Apple, Widevine for Android/Chrome, PlayReady for Microsoft — in the same file. One encrypted segment works for every device simultaneously.

In production

A streaming platform serves Roku, Android TV, Apple TV, Samsung Tizen, LG webOS, and their own devices. CMAF is almost certainly central to how they run one encoding pipeline that reaches all of these platforms without duplicating infrastructure or storage.
