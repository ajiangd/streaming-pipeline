01 · THE PROBLEM
Why streaming is hard
📡

Bandwidth varies wildly

A viewer's connection can go from 50 Mbps to 2 Mbps in seconds — commutes, crowded venues, bad WiFi. Fixed-quality video can't adapt. ABR can.

📱

Devices are everywhere

The same content needs to work on a 4K TV, a phone on LTE, a laptop on hotel WiFi. One fixed quality level can never serve all of these well.

⚡

Buffering is unacceptable

Studies show viewers abandon streams within seconds of a stall. ABR's goal is simple: play at the highest quality that will never buffer, and switch instantly if conditions change.
